About the Archive

YOU ARE ALREADY CLOSE TO PSYCHOPUNK.

If you feel the world around you is not as simple as it appears, you are already close to psychopunk.

Psychopunk is a way of seeing hidden systems beneath ordinary life: memory, identity, pressure, institutions, and the strange machinery that decides who gets to be seen.

In Revealed Cosmos, cities remember, bodies carry records, and private lives collide with systems that were never meant to be visible.

You can enter the archive through a video, a story, an image, a signal, or a strange dream that refuses to leave.
